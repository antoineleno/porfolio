campusstay is a website looking to change the way of campus hostel management


Our website aims to streamline campus hostel management by developing a centralized system for Student Affairs and Technical Staff to manage accommodation, student inquiries, maintenance requests, and leave applications. The system supports both male and female hostels, with multiple buildings for each type, and provides tools for assigning students to rooms, handling maintenance issues, and managing hostel leave.
